微結構和微發電機

The key components of the proposal can be divided into two parts: One is the brake-regenerative storage on nano hybrid system, the other one is the motoring operation and energy-saving via vector control of a high-efficiency motor. The system needs a Buck -Boost converter and a supper capacitor, and a high-efficiency FSPM (or PMA-SynRM) with gear device.
